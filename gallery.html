A taste of our Filipino heritage and culture

Our Signature Dishes

Mga pagkaing gawa sa puso — alaalang pampamilya, lutong tahanan, at kwentong Pilipino sa bawat hapag.

Binukadkad na Pla-Pla

Crispy whole tilapia butterflied and fried to golden perfection, served with native dipping sauce.

Kare-Kare

Traditional peanut stew with tender oxtail and vegetables, served with rich homemade bagoong.

Pancit sa Talangka

A comforting noodle special inspired by Kapampangan flavors—savory, smoky, and best shared.

Sizzling Sisig

Crispy and savory pork sizzling on a hot plate, balanced with citrus and spice—an iconic Filipino favorite.

Sinigang sa Ube

A modern twist on the classic sour stew, combining fresh vegetables and tender meat with the subtle creaminess of ube.

Halo-Halo

A refreshing medley of sweet preserves, shaved ice, and creamy leche flan—Filipino comfort in a glass.
