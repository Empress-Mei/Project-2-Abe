About our Restaurant

The Restaurant

Abe is the restaurant dedicated to the memory of artist-writer, gourmand and bon vivant E. Aguilar "Abe" Cruz, father of the late Larry Cruz. It serves traditional Filipino food, mostly Kapampangan, and gourmet dishes inspired by Abe's travels around the world.

Its concept and design provide the ideal setting for friends and family to dine comfortably and have a good time. Abe dishes are for sharing and served family-style.
